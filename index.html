Faster Forward Colorado (FFC) is a new initiative that partners with elected leaders to deliver outcomes on the issues that matter most to Coloradans. As an emerging 'think and do tank' we will accelerate progress on housing, child care, clean energy, transit and more, with the overarching message that we can lower costs and enable opportunity by moving faster to build what Colorado needs, while protecting what's best about our beautiful state.

The Opportunity

Why Colorado & Why Now

Colorado is a success story of Democratic governance — growing in population, attracting high-tech industry, and delivering innovative policymaking. Yet Coloradans face high costs for essentials like housing and child care, alongside slowing job growth. With a generational transition in statewide leadership in 2026, now is a high-leverage moment to shape what comes next.

Our Approach

Guiding Principles

Five design principles for policy action that drive faster impact and garner broad support.

01

Urgency

Speed to impact matters, and we should aspire to deliver in quarters, not years. Anything pushing out the time until people feel benefit needs strong justification.

02

Supply

To increase access and lower prices, we need to overcome artificial scarcity that forces Coloradans into cutthroat competition for essential needs.

03

Simplification

When we enlist the private sector to achieve public goals, we need to ensure processes and requirements don't work at cross-purposes to success.

04

Innovation

We should enable creativity and technology to unlock better ways to deliver for Coloradans across the public and private sectors.

05

Public Action

If the market isn't providing essential needs efficiently and fairly or is leaving people behind, governments shouldn't be afraid to take direct steps to deliver.

Where We Focus

Current Focus Areas

Housing & Transit
