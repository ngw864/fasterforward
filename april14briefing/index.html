Faster Forward Colorado & Aspect Strategies present

Virtual Briefing: Winning Majorities to Solve Affordability in Colorado

Including expert recap of the April 7 Lakewood special election

📅 Tuesday, April 14 · 3:00 PM MT
Please register and mark your calendar to join for a timely virtual briefing during which leaders of the Make Lakewood Livable campaign will recap the upcoming April 7 special election on Lakewood's ambitious housing affordability plan, in the context of research from Aspect Strategic exploring how key voting constituencies think about affordability solutions.

Coloradans consistently label cost of living — especially housing — as the top challenge they face and that they want elected leaders to address. Candidates focusing on housing have won local and state level elections and responded with action to grow housing supply and address affordability.

These policies have been proven effective in other cities and states. However, voter support for these policies falls far short of their support for pro-housing candidates they elect — drawing backlash including from core Democratic constituencies.

Lakewood, Colorado exemplifies this trend, electing a city council with a pro-housing supermajority that worked thoughtfully with constituents to craft and enact a housing affordability plan that will grow middle housing in the city. In response, a NIMBY organization forced a special election that will take place April 7 and could result in repeal of the policy.

If you are an elected leader, campaigner, advocate or policy expert interested in winning both the politics and policy of addressing cost of living… join Faster Forward Colorado and Aspect Strategies for a virtual briefing shortly after Lakewood's April 7 election to review the outcome, hear from campaign leaders on their strategies, share quantitative and qualitative research exploring voter attitudes, and explore how to build support for policies to address housing and affordability that extends all the way through to successful policy implementation.
